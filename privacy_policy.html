Privacy Policy

Introduction

WOYMMM respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you may share with us when using TimeParadox. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can control it. By using TimeParadox, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect and How It’s Used

User Data Stored Locally

TimeParadox does not collect or store user information on external servers. All player data, including scores, achievements, and gameplay records, is stored directly on your device and is inaccessible to us or any third-party service.

Contact Information

TimeParadox provides a customer support email address. If you contact us through this channel, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide to help resolve your inquiry

We will use this information solely for customer support and service-related purposes.

Advertising Information

We use Unity Ads as our third-party advertising provider. Unity Ads may collect data from your device to deliver personalized ads. Unity’s data collection may include:

  • Device information (e.g., device ID, IP address)
  • Location information (approximate or coarse location)
  • In-app interaction data for ad performance analysis

Refer to Unity's Privacy Policy for detailed information on the data they collect and how they use it.

How Your Information is Stored and Secured

Local Storage on Your Device

Gameplay data such as scores and achievements are stored only on your device. WOYMMM does not have access to or control over this data. If you uninstall TimeParadox, this locally stored data may be deleted as part of the uninstallation process.

Customer Support Data

Any contact information you provide to us through customer support is securely stored and accessed only for support purposes. This data will not be shared with third parties unless legally required.

Social Media Sharing

TimeParadox includes a share button within the game, enabling you to share content with social networks. Please note:

  • When you click on these social media widgets or share buttons, social media companies (such as Facebook, X, etc.) may collect information about you and your device.
  • This data collection and usage are governed by each social media provider’s privacy policy. For details, please review the policies of the respective networks with which you interact.

Use of Third-Party Advertising Services

Unity Ads is our primary third-party advertising service provider, and as such, they may collect and process data in accordance with their own policies. This data may be used to deliver ads tailored to your interests and measure ad performance.

Apple App Analytics and Crash Logging Tool

When you download and play TimeParadox from the Apple App Store, Apple may collect and analyze data regarding app usage, which can include:

  • App Analytics: Apple may provide us with aggregated statistics about app downloads, usage patterns, and user engagement metrics. This data helps us understand how our app is performing and how users interact with it.
  • Crash Logging Tool: If our app experiences a crash, Apple may collect crash reports to help us identify and fix issues. These reports are anonymized and do not contain personal information.

Data Retention

All data stored locally on your device will remain until you decide to uninstall the game or clear the app data.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Local Data Control: You have full control over the locally stored data on your device, including scores and achievements.
  • Customer Support Contact: If you wish to withdraw or request access to the information you provided to us for customer support, you may contact us at our provided support email.

Children's Privacy

We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13. All data generated by users, including scores and achievements, is stored locally on their devices and not transmitted to our servers.

We use Unity Ads as our advertising provider, which may collect data for the purpose of displaying personalized ads. Unity Ads adheres to its own privacy policies and practices, which may involve collecting usage data and device information. We encourage parents and guardians to review Unity's privacy policy for more information on how they handle data.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete such information from our records.

Parents or guardians should supervise their children's use of the app and ensure they understand our privacy practices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the app, and we encourage you to review this page regularly. Your continued use of TimeParadox signifies acceptance of any changes.

Contact Us
