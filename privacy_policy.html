Privacy Policy

Introduction

WOYMMM (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you may provide when using our games. This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes the types of information we collect, how such information is used, stored, and disclosed, and the choices you have regarding your information. By accessing or playing our games, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this Policy.

Information We Collect and How It Is Used

User Data Stored Locally

We do not collect or store any user information on external servers. All player data, including scores, achievements, and gameplay records, is stored locally on your device. Such data is inaccessible to us and any third-party services unless you choose to share it.

Contact Information

We provide a customer support email address. If you contact us through this channel, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name and email address
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide to assist in addressing your inquiry

We shall use this information solely for the purpose of providing customer support and shall not disclose it to third parties except as required by law.

Storage and Security of Your Information

Local Storage on Your Device

Gameplay data, such as scores and achievements, is stored only on your device. WOYMMM does not have access to or control over this data. Uninstallation of the game may result in deletion of this locally stored data.

Customer Support Data

Information provided to us for customer support purposes is stored securely and accessed solely for the purpose of responding to inquiries. Such information will not be shared with any third parties, except as required by applicable law or regulation.

Social Media Sharing

The game may include social media sharing features that allow you to share content with third-party social networks. Please note the following:

  • When you use these social media features, the corresponding social media companies (e.g., Facebook, X) may collect information about you and your device.
  • We do not control the data collection practices of these third parties. Please review the privacy policies of the social networks you interact with to understand how your data is handled.

Apple App Analytics and Crash Reporting

When you download and play our games via the Apple App Store, Apple may collect app usage and performance data, including:

  • App Analytics: Aggregated statistics regarding downloads, usage patterns, and engagement metrics to help us analyze app performance. Such data is anonymized.
  • Crash Reporting: In the event of an app crash, anonymized crash reports may be provided to us to assist in identifying and resolving technical issues.

Data Retention

All user data stored locally on your device will remain until you choose to uninstall the game or clear the app data. Customer support data shall be retained only for as long as necessary to provide support services, unless longer retention is required by law.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Local Data Control: You have full control over all data stored locally on your device, including scores and achievements.
  • Customer Support Contact: You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the information you provide for customer support by contacting us at the support email provided.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All gameplay data generated by children is stored locally and is not transmitted to our servers.

In the event we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we shall take steps to delete such information promptly.

Parents or guardians are responsible for supervising their children's use of the app and for understanding our privacy practices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app. Continued use of TimeParadox following the posting of updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.

Contact Us
